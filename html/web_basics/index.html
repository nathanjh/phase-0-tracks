The films of Rene Perez: A fansite

This site is dedicated to the films of auteur filmaker Rene Perez.

Elements of Style

  • Ever-so-slightly asynchronous dialogue conveys the ultimate futility of interpersonal commuincation as well as a deep sense of isolation.
  • Use of non-actors similar to Bresson. At times awkward, stunted, and uncomfortable performances create tension with the visual language; forcing the viewer to push through and ultimately transcend conventions of genre.
  • Wildly uneven pacing keeps the viewer disoriented--obscuring the narrative.
